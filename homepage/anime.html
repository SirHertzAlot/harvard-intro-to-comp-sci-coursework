How I got into Anime

Once upon a time, in the fantastical realm of childhood curiosity, a ten-year-old adventurer stumbled upon a treasure trove of animated wonders. Among the myriad of possibilities, one stood out like a gleaming jewel in the sun: "Vampire Hunter D." Little did this intrepid explorer know that this encounter would spark a lifelong odyssey through the vibrant cosmos of anime.

Armed with nothing but a voracious appetite for excitement and a trusty remote control, our intrepid anime aficionado scoured every corner of existence for the next adrenaline-pumping saga. From the dusty shelves of libraries to the boundless expanses of the internet, and even the enigmatic realms of ancient apps like WinAmp, no stone was left unturned in the quest for the next anime fix.

As the years rolled by, the collection of conquests grew, each anime adding a new layer of color to the tapestry of experiences. With over a thousand tales woven into the fabric of memory, our hero found solace and thrill in the realms of action and shonen anime, where battles raged, destinies clashed, and heroes rose from the ashes like phoenixes of legend.

List of Favorite Anime (Unordered version)

Among the pantheon of favorites, a constellation of legendary titles twinkled brightly: "Dr. Stone," where science and survival dance in an intricate duet of ingenuity; "Berserk," a dark symphony of blood and ambition echoing through the corridors of history; "Naruto," the epic saga of a young ninja's journey towards greatness and understanding; "Re:Zero," a labyrinth of time and fate where every step forward leads to a new twist of the knife; "Demon Slayer," a breathtaking ballet of blade and soul against the backdrop of a world in turmoil.

But the list doesn't end there, oh no! From the gothic majesty of "Hellsing Ultimate" to the alchemical wonders of "Full Metal Alchemist"; from the enchanting forests of "Princess Mononoke" to the whimsical flights of fancy in "Howl's Moving Castle" and "Kiki's Delivery Service"; from the mesmerizing dreamscapes of "Spirited Away" to the rugged frontier of "Fist of the North Star"; from the earth-shattering battles of "Attack on Titan" to the timeless adventures of "Dragon Ball," "Dragon Ball GT," and "Dragon Ball Z"; from the soul-stirring quests of "Hunter x Hunter" to the mind-bending machinations of "Death Note"; from the virtual realms of "Sword Art Online" and ".Hack//Sign" to the indomitable spirit of "Gurren Lagann" - each title a testament to the boundless creativity and unbridled passion that defines the world of anime.
